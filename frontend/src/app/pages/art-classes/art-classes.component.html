Age Groups & Why Join

Little Artists (Ages 3-6)

Introduction to basic art concepts through play-based learning. Focus on color recognition, simple shapes, and creative exploration using safe, child-friendly materials.

Young Creatives (Ages 7-12)

Development of fine motor skills and artistic techniques. Introduction to different art mediums including watercolors, pastels, and basic drawing skills.

Teen Artists (Ages 13-16)

Advanced techniques and personal artistic development. Focus on composition, color theory, and developing individual artistic style through guided instruction.
