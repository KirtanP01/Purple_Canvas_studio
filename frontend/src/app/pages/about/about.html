Ujwala Kanse

Founder, Purple Canvas Studio

Contact: ujwala.kanse@email.com Phone: +1 (224) 539-5188

Ujwala Kanse is a passionate acrylic and resin artist and the visionary behind Purple Canvas Studio, based in the Chicagoland area. With 5 years of professional experience, she brings a vibrant blend of technique, emotion, and storytelling to her work. Ujwala creates expressive acrylic paintings and striking resin pieces that explore texture, depth, and fluidity, capturing the essence of nature and human emotion. Her art has been featured in numerous galleries and private collections.

Beyond her own creations, Ujwala is dedicated to sharing her love for art with the community. She has conducted painting workshops at Harper College in Palatine and led creative events for all age groups, including children, seniors, and women. Through engaging art classes, community events, and art-themed birthday parties, she inspires creativity, self-expression, and meaningful connection.
